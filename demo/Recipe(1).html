Jollof Rice

Description

Jollof rice in a plate with salad and meat.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Onions
  • Groundnut oil
  • Pepper
  • Seasonings
  • Tomatoes
  • Scent leaf
  • Veggies
  • Meat

Steps

  • Clean your meat and put it in your pot
  • Add seasonings(salt,maggi,curry and thyme)
  • Cook till soft and fry in a hot pan of oil
  • Add the groundnut oil from frying the meat to the pot on the gas
  • Add the chopped onions to the semi hot oil
  • Add the pepper and tomatoes
  • Add meat broth if available
  • Add the seasonings (Salt,maggi,curry and thyme)
  • Allow to fry for 10 mins
  • Wash the rice with salt and add to the fried pepper mix
  • Add enough water to cook
  • Cook until soft and dish
  • Add the salad mix to the dished plate of rice
