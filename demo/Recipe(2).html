Pancakes

Description

A plate of pancakes with honey and cube of butter.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Eggs
  • Pinch of salt
  • Milk
  • Melted butter
  • Cooking oil
  • Honey

Steps

  • In a large bowl,whisk together : flour,sugar,baking powder and salt.
  • In a separate bowl,whisk together : milk,egg and melted butter.
  • Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.Do not overmix.
  • Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat.Grease with butter or cooking oil.
  • Drop a little portion of the batter measured with a small cup,onto the oan.
  • Cook for 2-3 minutes,until bubbles appear on the surface and edges start to dry.
  • Flip and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes,until golden brown.
  • Serve warm with your favourite topping such as butter,honey,syrup,whipped cream.e.t.c.
